Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Welcome to Spam Not Spam. This Privacy Policy explains how our Real-Time Spam Mail Detection application collects, uses, and protects your data. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

📧 Information We Access

Our application uses the Google Gmail API to read email message content for the purpose of detecting spam messages. We only request the following permission:

🔐 Gmail Read-Only Access

The application does not modify, send, delete, or alter any emails. We maintain read-only access to ensure your emails remain completely safe and untouched.

🔍 How We Use Your Data

Email data accessed through Gmail is used exclusively for spam detection using advanced machine learning models. Here's what we do with your information:

  • Analyze email content to detect spam patterns
  • Generate spam detection predictions
  • Temporarily process data for classification
  • Never permanently store sensitive email content
  • Never share data with third parties

💾 Data Storage

We store minimal information required to run the service efficiently:

What We Store: Only spam detection prediction results (classification as spam or not spam) are retained for service improvement and historical records.

What We Don't Store: Sensitive email content, message bodies, attachments, and personal identifiable information are not permanently stored on our servers.

🌐 Third-Party Services

This application uses external services to provide functionality:

  • Google APIs: For Gmail authentication and email access. Use is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Backend Services: For processing spam detection requests.

We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

🛡️ Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your information:

  • Use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Implement authentication mechanisms through Google OAuth
  • Limit data access to only necessary functionality
  • Regularly review and update security practices
Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. Users should take personal precautions to protect their account credentials.

🔄 Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Revoke application access to your Gmail account at any time through Google Account settings
  • Request information about what data we hold
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the application following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means you accept and agree to the changes.
